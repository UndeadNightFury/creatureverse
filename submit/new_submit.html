Introduction

So you want to submit your own page to the story? Well there are a few rules and guidelines you must follow in order to be approved! If you want to start your own story world instead Click Here for the needed template or if your interested in submitting your own original character Click Here for the needed template.

Rules

  1. All submissions must follow the template layout! Click Here
  2. No sex. (Off scene or implied is ok.)
  3. No AI Generated Text! (if caught you WILL be blacklisted from ever submitting again!!)
  4. No Religious content. (Made up religions can be approved by me via CreatureVerse Forums.)
  5. No Offensive language.

How to submit

If you agree to follow the rules laid out above you can send your submission to submit@creatureverse.net Click Here, on a Google Form Click Here, on the CreatureVerse Official Discord Server Click here, or on the CreatureVerse Forums Click Here!
