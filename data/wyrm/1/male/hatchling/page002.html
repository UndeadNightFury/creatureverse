Seeing two of your siblings play fighting nearby, you decided to tackle the blue-scaled wyrmling, whom, upon landing, squealed in surprise and accidentally whipped her tail around, hitting you in the right eye. You roar of pain attracted the attention of your mother who quickly picked you up and placed you on a large stone.

"What happened, little one?" she cooed to you as she examined your injured eye. As she examined your injured eye she carefully cleans the blood from your eye causing you whimper in pain and discomfort. "Oh my poor little hatchling!" Your mother said softly, "Your eye is damaged beyond repair!"
