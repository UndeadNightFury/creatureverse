As you lay there dying gasping and spitting up blood as you struggle for life in vain, you know without a heart beating inside your chest you will die. You glance up at the rotting dragon who ripped your precious heart from your chest cavity and realize there was a way to survive.

"P-ple-pleas..." You notice the dragons head turn towards you as you struggle to speak between gasps of breath and choaking on your blood.

Before you can make another attempt to speak your forelegs give out and you collaspse onto your right side. Darkness begins to fill your vision when the dragon leans down and looks you in the eyes.

"You wish for survival?" The dragon questions, its voice rattling.

"Are you willing to do anything for to survive?" He asked you as you stared into his lifeless golden eyes...
