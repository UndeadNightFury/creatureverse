You lean down to examine the wound noting how a bit of your intestines are sliping through. You carefully grabbed the organ and were about to start pushing it back in when the world suddenly started spinning as you were falling to the ground.

Landing with a soundless grunt you stared up at a headless draconic body that was tugging its own intestines out in its death throws. A minute later it registered with your brain that you were looking at your own body, you had been decapitated!

Your last view before you fell unconscious was of a rotting foreleg implaing your twitching corpse and ripping out your still beating heart...
