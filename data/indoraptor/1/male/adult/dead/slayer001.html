You stare in horror as the vital organ struggles valiantly for life while gripped by Slayer. A moment later Slayer opens his paw and drops the organ to the ground and walks away, leaving you for dead. While you fight valiantly to remain alive you are slowly failing as darkness begins to cloud your vision; your labored breathing slows to a stop and you weakly gurgle out a bit of blood from the back of your throat. The last thing you are aware of is that your heart had stopped beating...
