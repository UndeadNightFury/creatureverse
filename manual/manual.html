Night Fury

The Night Fury is one of the most powerful dragons of the world, it remains one of the most popular species of dragon of modern times!

Abilities

  1. 1. Plasma Blasts

  2. 2. Echolocation

  3. 3. Retractable Teeth

  4. 4. Lightning Camouflage

History

Having evolved as nocturnal ambush predators Night Furies have smouth jet black scales, their bodies have evovled to make them the perfect fighter in the air able to break the sound barrier with a little effert. Sadly the night fury has been hunted to near total extinction by humans and other dragons.

Light Fury

The Light Fury has yet to be implemented into CreatureVerse. However like the Night Fury, the Light Fury is one of the most powerful dragons of CreatureVerse! The Light Fury has the most beautiful snow white scales ever!

Abilities

  1. 1. Plasma Blasts

  2. 2. Cloaking

History

Similar to the Night Fury, the Light Fury evloved as a ambush predator with a slieek body of pure snowy white scales that rediate beauty and grace. Sadly like the night fury the light fury has been hunted to near extinction with very few members left of their species.

Water Elf

A water elf otherwise known as a sea elf, merwoman, merman, mermaid are descendants of the high elves after the split off ages ago to form their own society in the oceans. The Water Elf is not yet implemented into CreatureVerse

Abilities

  1. Some Water Elves can transform their tails into legs to walk on land though this ability is very rare.

  2. Water Elves have a natual mastery of the element of water!

History

Long ago a small tribe of high elves split off from the main group and formed their own society of elves within the oceans sacrificing their legs for fish tails and their lungs for gills.
