Night Fury

The Night Fury is one of the most powerful dragons of the world, it remains one of the most popular species of dragon of modern times!

Abilities

  1. 1. Plasma Blasts

  2. 2. Echolocation

  3. 3. Retractable Teeth

  4. 4. Lightning Camouflage

History

Having evolved as nocturnal ambush predators Night Furies have smooth jet black scales, their bodies have evovled to make them the perfect fighter in the air and able to break the sound barrier with little effort. Sadly the night fury has been hunted to near total extinction by humans and other dragons.

Velociraptor

The Velociraptor or Raptor was one of the smartest saurians to have ever lived, raptors had a variety of different scale colors with forest green being the most common and black being very rare.

Abilities

  1. 1. Smell prey up to 1 mile away

  2. 2. Disembowel prey

  3. 3. Run up to 40 mph

History

The Velociraptors were one of the smartest saurians species to have ever existed, having grown fully sentient in the first 2 million years of their existence they used that to make complex stratageies against their prey.

Air Elf

The Air Elves: Also known as Sky Elves were winged elves that had huge feathery wings, they had a unmatched mastery over air elemental magic than any other species.

Abilities

  1. 1. Feathered Wings

  2. 2. Mastery over Air Elemental Magic

History

Ages ago the Sky Elves broke off from the High Elves and moved to a very unique planet, the planet they settled on was special because of the many floating islands dotting the entire world. For millions of years they lived in peace gaining their famous feathered wings. Sadly today the Sky Elves are compeletly extinct having been wiped out millions of years ago in a great cataclysm.
