Ribs the Indoraptor

Name: Ribs

Age: 14

Species: Indoraptor

Gender: Male

Pack: First Indoraptor Pack - Hunter

Description: Ribs is a young skinny black scaled indoraptor with a bright yellow stripe along his back on either side, bright yellow eyes with reptilian pupils. Ribs is eager to prove himself to his alpha after he was saved from the lab, Ribs was named due to a genetic defect that caused him to always remain skinny.

Tank the Indoraptor

Name: Tank

Age: 23

Species: Indoraptor

Gender: Male

Pack: First Indoraptor Pack - Hunter

Description: Tank is a massive hulking indoraptor with crimson stripes running along his back on either side, red eyes with reptilian pupils. Tank earned his name for being able to withstand massive blows that would have normally killed any living creature.

Ripper the Indoraptor

Name: Ripper

Age: 20

Species: Indoraptor

Gender: Male

Pack: First Indoraptor Pack - Enforcer

Description: Ripper is a psychotic red scaled indoraptor with pure black eyes lacking pupils. Ripper is extremely sadistic taking pleasure with playing with his prey before he eats them alive, he was subjected to many experiments by humans at the lab twisting him into a demonic monster of a indoraptor. While Ripper is a very evil creature he is also extremely loyal to his alpha who saved him from the nightmare of the lab.

David the Indoraptor

Name: David

Age: 9

Species: Indoraptor

Gender: Male

Pack: First Indoraptor Pack - Hunter

Description: Among the youngest rescued from the lab, David has snow white scales with black stripes running down his back on either side, he has bright blue eyes with reptilian pupils. David is one of the only indoraptors with a human name, having been the favorite of one of the scientists.

Slayer the Indoraptor

Name: Slayer

Age: 15

Species: Indoraptor

Gender: Male

Pack: First Indoraptor Pack - Soldier

Description: Slayer is a very hateful person who like Ripper is extremely sadistic taking pleasure in torturing his prey. Slayer also has a deep seeded hatred of the Alpha due to him getting preferential treatment in the labs. He is one of the First Indoraptor Packs best soldiers.
