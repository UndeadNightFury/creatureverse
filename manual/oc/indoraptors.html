Ribs the Indoraptor

Name: Ribs

Age: 14

Gender: Male

Pack: The First Indoraptor Pack - Hunter

Description: Ribs is a young skinny black scaled indoraptor with a bright yellow stripe along his back on either side, bright yellow eyes with reptilian pupils. Ribs is eager to prove himself to his alpha after he was saved from the lab, Ribs was named due to a genetic defect that caused him to always remain skinny.

Gender: Male

Pack: The First Indoraptor Pack - Enforcer

Ripper is extremely sadstic taking plesure with playing with his prey before he eats them alive, he was subjected to many experiments by humans at the lab twisting him into a demonic monster of a indoraptor.
